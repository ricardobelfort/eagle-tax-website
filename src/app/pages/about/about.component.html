Sobre Nós

Our Journey

Founded in 1993 by Paulo Oliveira and his wife Priscilla in Coconut Creek, Florida, Eagle Tax began as a small home office with a handful of clients. With Paulo’s background as a CPA in Brazil and Priscilla’s expertise in accounting and linguistics, they quickly established a reputation for excellence in tax preparation and accounting services. Their commitment to the Brazilian community and beyond allowed them to grow significantly, serving over 2,500 clients across the U.S. and various countries, including Argentina, Colombia, Canada, and Portugal.

Over the years, Eagle Tax has expanded its offerings to include a comprehensive range of solutions such as international and expatriate tax services, IRS dispute resolution, bookkeeping, payroll, and more. Their dedication to quality and community service made them a trusted name in the industry.

A New Chapter

In 2021, after nearly three decades of successful service, Paulo and Priscilla decided to retire. Seeking a successor who could uphold their legacy and further enhance the company’s offerings, they found the ideal candidate in Alvin Hommerding. With extensive experience as a CPA and a strong background in auditing and financial management, Alvin took over Eagle Tax in October 2022.

Under Alvin’s leadership, the firm has embraced new technologies and innovative practices, ensuring that our team is well-equipped to meet the evolving needs of our clients. With a commitment to maintaining the high standards set by Paulo and Priscilla, we have strengthened our team and expanded our capabilities.

Our Commitment

At Eagle Tax, we are passionate about creating value for our clients by addressing their most challenging financial concerns. Our solutions portfolio includes tax return preparation, international and expatriate tax services, IRS dispute resolution, bookkeeping, payroll, workers’ compensation audits, and paralegal services for LLC and corporation management.

We pride ourselves on our core values of professionalism, integrity, transparency, and a client-centric approach. Our goal is to foster long-term, win-win relationships, ensuring our clients thrive in today’s global marketplace.

As we continue to build on the legacy of Paulo and Priscilla, we are dedicated to providing unparalleled service and expertise, helping our clients navigate their financial journeys with confidence.
